Play Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below: by clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The vavlue of each crystal is hidden from you until you click it.

Each time when the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
